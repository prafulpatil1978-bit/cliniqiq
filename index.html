Failure mode drives the entire licensing strategy. Safety-failed assets are uninvestable. Efficacy-failed assets may be endpoint-redesignable. Commercial-failed assets may suit emerging markets. This cross-TA comparison is decision-critical.
BD Implication: CNS has 58% efficacy failure rate — highest of all TAs. This makes CNS licensing decisions primarily about endpoint design quality, not just safety. Cardiovascular has the highest safety failure rate (28%) — AE burden in MACE populations is the critical due diligence lens.
